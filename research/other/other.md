This research repository focuses on LinkedIn organic growth strategies in B2B SaaS.
Across multiple expert sources, a clear pattern emerges:
- High-performing LinkedIn content is distribution-first, not algorithm-first  
- Audience attention is the core targeting unit, not keywords or hashtags  
- Strong SaaS creators focus on narrative + opinion, not product features  
- Growth is driven by repetition, consistency, and personal authority building  
- The most effective posts are derived from real operator experience, not theory  
This repository is designed to later form a practical LinkedIn growth playbook for SaaS founders and marketers.
