# Video Info
- Title: How To Grow ANY Business FASTER (Masterclass)
- Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qsXxckCbci0
- Topic: Structural framework for understanding business archetypes  
- Creator: Alex Hormozi (Acquisition.com)
## Main Ideas
- All businesses fall into 4 models: e-commerce, services, education, SaaS  
- Each model has a distinct constraint (capital, talent, churn, retention)  
- Constraints are features of the model, not problems  
- Brand is the universal moat across all models  
- Misdiagnosis of model leads to scaling failure  
## Key Insights
- "Features vs bugs" is the central diagnostic framework  
- Retention is the defining line between SaaS and info businesses  
- Education businesses naturally create their own competition  
- SaaS value = friction removal, not feature expansion  
- Brand strategy differs across models but is always essential  
## SaaS / Business Application
- Do not scale acquisition before retention validation  
- Treat churn as structural signal, not operational failure  
- Build virality into product architecture early  
- Service businesses must systematize hiring and delivery  
- E-commerce must balance brand vs direct response spend  
## Summary
Every business fits a structural model with predictable constraints. Growth failures come from misdiagnosing those constraints rather than fixing them.
