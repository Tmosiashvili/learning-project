# Video Info
- Title: Sales Therapy with Kyle Poyar (Growth Unhinged, OpenView)
- Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3aq2dThwhIE
- Topic: How SaaS growth evolves in the AI era and what modern GTM looks like
## Main Ideas
- SaaS playbooks may not fully apply to AI-native companies
- Market saturation increases competition and raises bar for differentiation
- Growth is shifting from corporate brand → creator + community-led engines
- Content → community → product → feedback loop becomes core growth system
- ICP clarity is more important than scale
- GTM is now hybrid: PLG + sales + community + content combined
- Buying process is more complex (multiple stakeholders)
- Personal brands outperform company brands in distribution
## Key Insights
- Openly sharing thinking improves decision quality via feedback loops
- Community is now a pre-product asset, not post-PMF layer
- Trust shifts from companies → individuals
- GTM is no longer linear but multi-system interconnected
- AI may break old SaaS assumptions entirely
- Influence matters more than authority in modern ecosystems
## Psychological Angle
- Fear of irrelevance in fast-changing AI market
- Curiosity about unknown GTM future
- Belonging to “people figuring it out together”
- Authority through transparency and exploration
- Social proof via creators and founders
## SaaS Application
- Build founder-led or employee-led content engines
- Treat community as growth channel, not branding
- Combine PLG + sales for expansion
- Build feedback loops from customers → product → content
- Focus heavily on ICP clarity before scaling
## Short Summary
SaaS growth is shifting toward community-driven, creator-led ecosystems where distribution and trust matter more than traditional company branding.
